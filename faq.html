Frequently Asked Questions

  • How many lessons do I need to do my license?
    You require a minimum of 12 driving lessons to attempt your drivers license test. You should also be practising your driving in between lessons.
  • Why should I do automatic lessons?
    Automatic and electric cars are much easier to drive, and they are becoming more common - it is good to plan for the future.
  • Will i be covered by insurance?
    You will have full insurance for the duration of your driving lessons.
  • What car will be used for my lessons?
    Lessons are given in a fully electric 2022 Renault Zoe
