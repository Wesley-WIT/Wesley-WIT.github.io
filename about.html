About Us

Steven O'Donnell is a veteran driver, with over 20 years of experience driving trucks, buses and cars. He is now an RSA-approved driving instructor, offering automatic driving lessons. Steven is a patient but attentive instructor, offering sound advice on your journey to becoming a fully licensed driver. Already licensed drivers looking to brush up on their abilities will find Steven's years of experience to be of great benefit as well.

Hush School of Motoring offers automatic driving lessons with a unique twist - they're all done in a fully electric Renault Zoe.

Why Electric?

The cost of running an electric vehicle is much lower than that of a traditional internal combustion engine vehicle. This saving is reflected in our prices - we offer the lowest driving lesson rates in the country, with price-matching!

The zero-carbon experience of an electric car is very unique. Brakes are more responsive than traditional cars, and the low center of gravity makes it nearly impossible to roll. Not to mention the risk of fatality in a motor accident is lower in electric vehicles than in hybrid or traditional combustion engine cars.
